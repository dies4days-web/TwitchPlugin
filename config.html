Friends Follow Extension Configuration

Setup Instructions for Streamers

Welcome! This extension lets you create a curated list of Twitch friends (other streamers) that your viewers can easily follow with one click from a slide-out panel on your video player.

  1. Add Friends: Enter a Twitch username (e.g., "xai" without the @) in the input field below and click "Add Friend". You can add as many as you like.
  2. Manage List: Your current list appears below. Click "Remove" next to any username to delete it.
  3. Save Changes: After making edits, click "Save List" to update. Changes will appear in your stream's viewer panel immediately.
  4. Viewer Experience: On your stream, viewers will see a "Friends" tab on the right side of the player. Clicking it slides out a panel with your list, each with a "Follow" button that triggers Twitch's native follow prompt.
  5. Tips: Use valid Twitch usernames only. The panel is non-intrusive and hides during ads. If you have questions, check the Twitch Extensions Dashboard or contact the developer.

This setup is unique to your channel—other streamers using this extension will have their own lists.
